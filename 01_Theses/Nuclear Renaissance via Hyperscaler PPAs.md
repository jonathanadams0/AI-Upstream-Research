# Nuclear Renaissance via Hyperscaler PPAs

---

## The Pitch

Hyperscalers need dispatchable, carbon-free baseload to hit both their power and sustainability commitments. Nuclear is the only asset class that fits. The Constellation-Microsoft Three Mile Island deal re-priced the option value of restarts, and the Amazon-Talen and Google-Kairos deals extended the thesis to SMRs. This re-rates existing nuclear operators and creates structural uranium demand.

---

## Why Now

- 2024-2025 PPA announcements set new pricing benchmarks
- SMR commercial deployments targeting 2028-2030 operation
- Federal support (IRA PTC, DOE loan programs)
- Uranium contracting cycle turning

---

## Supporting Evidence

- CEG / MSFT TMI PPA reportedly at premium to merchant
- Amazon's Talen / Cumulus deal (behind-the-meter at Susquehanna)
- Oklo, NuScale, X-energy all advancing

## What Would Invalidate This

1. Major nuclear incident
2. Hyperscaler capex moderation
3. Breakthrough in grid-scale storage making renewables dispatchable
4. SMR cost/schedule failures (NuScale precedent)

---

## Time Horizon

- Near-term: Continued PPA announcements, uranium contracting
- Medium-term: Restart economics for shuttered plants (Palisades, Duane Arnold)
- Long-term: SMR deployment success/failure determines magnitude

## Review Note (2026-06-23)

Thesis reviewed against AI capex deceleration scenario. Key distinction: **existing PPAs are already signed** — the TMI/Microsoft, Amazon/Talen, and Google/Kairos deals are long-term contracts unaffected by quarterly AI capex sentiment. CEG and VST's contracted nuclear economics are not at risk.

The element most sensitive to capex deceleration is **new PPA announcement cadence** — if hyperscalers slow AI spending, fewer new nuclear deals may be announced in H2'26. This affects the sentiment narrative but not the fundamental earnings of nuclear fleet operators already contracted.

SMR commercialization (2028-2030 target dates) operates on a timeline far beyond quarterly capex cycles and is driven by federal support (IRA PTC, DOE loan programs) more than hyperscaler mood.
