# CDU — Coolant Distribution Unit

A Coolant Distribution Unit (CDU) is the central hub of a direct liquid cooling (DLC) system in a data center. It circulates chilled water or glycol coolant from the facility-level chiller to rack-level cold plates on GPUs and CPUs, then returns warmed coolant to the chiller for heat rejection.

**Components:** Pump, heat exchanger, flow control valves, temperature sensors, leak detection
**Typical cooling capacity:** 100-500 kW per CDU
**Fluid:** Distilled water + corrosion inhibitors, or propylene glycol mixture

**Why CDUs are bottlenecked:** Each new AI campus requires hundreds of CDUs. Vertiv is the primary CDU supplier; lead times have extended to 12-18+ months in 2024-2025. The CDU market is the clearest real-time signal for AI infrastructure capex.
