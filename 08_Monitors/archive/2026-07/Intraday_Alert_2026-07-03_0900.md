1. **Michael Burry short disclosures** — Burry published a Substack post (July 2) disclosing short positions against MU, AMAT, and NVDA, calling Micron a structural "destroyer of capital" and shorted MU at ~$1,051. Widely amplified across financial media July 2–3.

3. **Fed QT resumption** — New Fed Chair Kevin Warsh signaled resumption of quantitative tightening (balance sheet reduction), adding pressure to long-duration / high-multiple tech.
4. **Photonics sector reset** — AAOI -17%, COHR and LITE both -10% in a sector-wide repricing; no single company-specific catalyst identified, characterized by the press as a "valuation reset" for high-multiple optical names.

**Consensus framing:** Multiple Seeking Alpha pieces describe this as "rotation, not recession" — Dow industrials at records, semis taking the pain. None of the moves appear to implicate company-specific thesis breaks across the watchlist.
