Burry posted on Substack (dated July 2) a new short in MU (shorted at $1,051.87 on July 1) and disclosed existing shorts in NVDA and AMAT. Sentiment amplifier, not a fundamental change — but added a vol spike to names already under pressure.

**4. Weak June jobs report (57K NFP)**
Macro catalyst for the rotation. Soft payrolls reinforced a "AI capex pausing / real economy slowing" narrative and triggered growth-to-value rotation.
