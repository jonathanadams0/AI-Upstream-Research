## One-Line Verdict

The AI storage supercycle is real and SNDK is the purest play on it — but at $2,182 (34.7x FY2026E peak-cycle EPS), the stock prices in a best-case scenario and the probability-weighted expected value (~$1,612) is 26% below the current price.

---

## Five Key Tensions

1. **New Business Model vs. Same Old Cycle** — $42B NBM backlog suggests structural visibility, but NAND has crashed 60–80% gross margin before; Samsung's supply discipline is unconfirmed.
2. **Parabolic Trajectory vs. Peak-Cycle Danger** — GM expanded from 22.5% → 78.4% in four quarters. Whether that's a new baseline or a one-way trip to the mean is the central question.
3. **AI Datacenter Is Real vs. Still Only 24.7% of Revenue** — Edge (smartphones, PCs) is 61.6% of revenue and more cyclical than the bull narrative acknowledges.
4. **Kioxia JV Is Manufacturing Scale vs. Your JV Partner Is Your Competitor** — Locked through 2034; Kioxia competes for the same hyperscaler NBM agreements.
5. **Stock Is 18% Above Consensus PT vs. Sell Side Still Mostly Buy** — 14 Buy / 2 Hold; avg PT $1,843 (below current). Market has run past analysts.
