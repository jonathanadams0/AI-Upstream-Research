Three updates that change the watch framework:

**3. Micron signed a multi-year EUV supply agreement at 1δ node (June 24)**
Announced during Micron's Q3 FY2026 earnings call. This is a named, publicly disclosed downstream demand signal for future EUV tools. Watch for ASML to reference it explicitly in customer commentary.
