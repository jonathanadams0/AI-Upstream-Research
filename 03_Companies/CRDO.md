# Credo Technology (CRDO)

> Active Electrical Cables (AECs) for short-reach AI cluster connections. Revenue grew 206% YoY in FY2026. Credo solves a specific pain point: the 1-3 meter connections between GPUs and switches within a rack or between adjacent racks — too short for optical, too far for passive copper at 800G.

**Sector:** [[AI-Networking]] | **Rating:** Hold | **PT:** $250 | **Current:** ~$267 (June 23 intraday) | **Downside:** -6%

---

## 2. Thesis

AECs replace passive Direct Attach Copper (DAC) cables at 800G+ speeds. At AI cluster density (1-3m rack-to-rack), optical is overkill (too expensive, too complex) and DAC doesn't work (too lossy). AECs have active retimers that clean the signal — enabling 800G over copper at short reach. Credo dominates this niche.

---

## 3. Hold Rationale

At $228 and ~37x revenue (FY2026 revenue ~$1.34B, market cap ~$50B), the extraordinary growth is priced. Revenue growing 200%+ is exceptional; sustaining it as competition intensifies is the question. Hold/$250. Would become a Buy on any correction to ~$180.
