**Q1 — Any named external 18A foundry customer beyond Apple?**
The PT Reset Branch A is explicitly gated on "foundry catalyst lands (named external 18A customer, or foundry gross margin inflection)." The Trump-announced Apple deal was unconfirmed at initiation. A formal 8-K or call disclosure naming a hyperscaler (NVIDIA, Google, Meta, Amazon) on 18A/14A wafer orders is the highest-conviction positive signal possible. **If it lands → Branch A (re-run probability-weighted EV, new PT).**

**Q2 — DCAI segment: is the +22% YoY trajectory maintained?**
Q1 DCAI was $5.1B (+22% YoY) on Xeon 6 and Gaudi 3 demand. Q2 DCAI is the cleanest measure of whether Intel is winning real data center revenue vs. AMD Epyc. A deceleration to +10% or below would be a structural concern; continuation at +15%+ confirms the AI inference CPU narrative.

**Q3 — FCF trajectory and cash burn pace**
FCF was −$3.1B TTM at initiation (improving from −$4.9B in FY2025). $45B debt and $12B+ annual capex means cash burn is the key solvency check until revenue scales. Any improvement toward breakeven FCF (even −$1–2B TTM) accelerates the bull thesis; any re-widening would raise the probability of the bear case.
