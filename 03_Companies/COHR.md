# Coherent Corp (COHR)

> The largest supplier of optical transceivers for AI data centers. Coherent surged 17.6% on June 2 to a record $426 on AI infrastructure announcements at Computex. The company makes the 800G and 1.6T transceivers that physically connect GPUs to switches in AI clusters. At AI cluster scale, optical interconnect is the bottleneck.

## 2. Thesis

1. **Optical transceivers are a genuine AI bottleneck.** 800G transceivers for AI clusters sold out in 2024-2025. Coherent controls a large share of AI-grade 800G production. Every 100K-GPU cluster requires 100K+ optical links.

2. **Speed race benefits Coherent.** 400G → 800G → 1.6T → 3.2T. Each generation requires new transceiver designs where Coherent's R&D lead matters.

3. **Compound photonics.** Coherent's silicon photonics + traditional laser chip capabilities enable next-gen co-packaged optics — where transceivers are integrated directly onto switch chips, eliminating pluggable module cost.
