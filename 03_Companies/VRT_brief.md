# Vertiv Holdings (VRT) — 30-Day Company Brief
**Date:** June 3, 2026 | **Rating:** Buy | **PT:** $370 | **Current:** $331.44

---

## Summary

Vertiv is in "hyper-growth mode" (RBC's language) with no visible deceleration in the demand signal that matters most: data center liquid cooling orders. The company held its first major investor day in ~18 months on May 19, unveiled 120 product innovations, outlined 2030 targets implying 20-22% organic revenue growth, and launched a NVIDIA partnership for integrated AI factory infrastructure design. The stock is +95% YTD and +195% over 1 year — but still 11% below analyst consensus mean PT of $371.67 (18 Buy, 1 Hold, 0 Sell).

## Key Developments (May 5 – June 3, 2026)

**May 15 — RBC raises PT to $435**
RBC Capital Markets estimated Vertiv growing topline by at least 20-25% organically through 2030 (mid-to-high-20s CAGR for EPS), citing 12 years of implied US datacenter construction backlog maintaining "demand outstripping supply." Maintained Outperform. Source: [MT Newswires, May 15, 2026](https://app.bigdata.com/documents/C146528B9CF98BE4A39CBE0AF16C3AF6?cnum=1)

**May 19 — Investor Day: 120 innovations, 2030 targets**
CEO Giordano Albertazzi unveiled 120 product innovations taken to market over the last 18 months: power converters handling AI's dynamic/spiky loads, battery energy storage, liquid cooling (chillers, DLC), SmartRun prefab systems, OneCore converged infrastructure, Waylay AI-powered predictive maintenance. 2030 targets: 20-22% organic revenue growth, 30% incremental margins. CEO: "Infrastructure density increases and deployment timelines compress, we're positioned to be the partner customers need to bring their most ambitious projects to life, at scale." Source: [Quartr Transcripts, May 19, 2026](https://app.bigdata.com/documents/D7FC7CFB7C5756052D23FFD652E96801?cnum=26)

**May 21 — RBC follow-up: "Some conservatism baked in"**
RBC published second note after investor day, noting the 2030 targets embed conservatism — bull case $21-22 EPS by 2030 at 30-35% incremental margins vs. management's 30% assumption. RBC maintained Outperform/$435 PT. Source: [MT Newswires, May 21, 2026](https://app.bigdata.com/documents/18A44BB4039F5F2D12984C7275834BC3?cnum=1)

**May 28 — CEO: "800V DC is the next wave"**
In various investor communications, management flagged the industry-wide transition to 800V DC architecture as the next structural expansion of VRT's TAM. 800V DC will expand liquid cooling requirements across the entire IT stack (not just GPUs), with product prototypes launching in late 2026. Delivery windows "a little bit more elongated" at 12-18 months — providing earnings visibility well into 2027. Source: [The Globe And Mail, May 28, 2026](https://app.bigdata.com/documents/C9EB63C704899E6ECA7F8115EFBF60CB?cnum=3)

**June 1 — NVIDIA partnership: converged AI factory digital twin**
Vertiv introduced the first converged physical infrastructure digital twin for NVIDIA Omniverse DSX — standardized 12.5 MW modular building blocks, Vertiv OneCore, and system-level designs integrating power, cooling, and controls. Goal: reduce deployment complexity, speed up commissioning, enable "digitally validated, high-performance AI infrastructure from the grid to the chip level." Source: [PubT, June 1, 2026](https://app.bigdata.com/documents/3D15DC703374216142A4BE609333AEC5)

**June 3 — PurgeRite NearZero service launch**
Vertiv launched PurgeRite NearZero, a patent-pending fluid management service using reverse osmosis and water recycling to minimize water sourcing and waste during liquid cooling system commissioning. Addresses regulatory and operational challenges as AI campuses scale toward GW-class deployments. Also declared Q2 dividend of $0.0625/share (payable June 25). Source: [Benzinga, June 3, 2026](https://app.bigdata.com/documents/8000F00ECDB470E71467FA27D476CBBD?cnum=2)

## Investment View

The Q4 2025 order surge (+252% organic) and Q1 2026 margin beat (+180 bps above guidance) confirm that Vertiv is executing through what should be a complex scaling environment. The NVIDIA partnership and 800V DC transition commentary suggest management sees the next phase of demand (higher-density, more complex AI architectures) as an expansion of their addressable market — not a saturation of the current one.

At $331 with consensus PT at $372 (11% upside), VRT is the most directly AI-exposed pure-play in the coverage universe at a reasonable discount to analyst expectations. RBC's bull case EPS of $21-22 in 2030 vs. 2026 guidance of high-single-digit EPS implies a multi-year compounding story at current stock levels.

**PT: $370. Rating: Buy. Conviction: High.**
