## 2. Context

Wolfspeed was formerly Cree, a LED and SiC materials company. It spun off its LED business and rebranded as Wolfspeed to focus on SiC. It operates the world's largest SiC substrate facility in Durham, NC, and the "Mohawk Valley" SiC device fab in Marcy, NY (the largest SiC device facility in the world at the time of construction).

**The thesis:** SiC substrates are the chokepoint for the global SiC device supply chain. If you own the substrates, you control supply.

**The execution problem:** The Mohawk Valley fab ramp has been slower than expected, requiring significant capital. Wolfspeed has burned through significant capital and has had to navigate through periods of distressed financing conditions.

**June 2026 pivot:** Wolfspeed launched a dedicated team to sell SiC power solutions to AI data center operators. The pitch: SiC enables more efficient power conversion at 120kW+ AI rack densities, reducing data center power consumption and cooling costs.
