## 10. Bear Case

**Scenario:** GEV/Kiewit CCGT pipeline fails to convert any LOIs to signed PPAs within 12 months. Net debt/EBITDA remains above 5.5x due to LS Power integration friction. ERCOT power prices structurally weaken below $40/MWh.

**Implied Bear Price:** $110 (~17% downside from ~$132 current)

**Change Rating to Hold/Sell If:**
1. **No GEV/Kiewit LOI converts to signed PPA within 12 months.** Management has stated LOIs increased 35% QoQ through Q3 2025. If by June 2027 no LOI has converted to a signed long-term contract, the $2.5B EBITDA pipeline optionality collapses to zero.
2. **Net debt/EBITDA does not decline below 5.0x by YE2026.** Post-LS Power net debt is ~$27B (~4.8x 2026E EBITDA). If EBITDA misses guidance by >10% for two consecutive quarters, leverage looks structural rather than transitional.
3. **ERCOT spot prices average below $40/MWh for a full calendar quarter.** NRG's Texas gross margin is the primary earnings lever. Guidance assumes ~$52/MWh; sustained sub-$40 print triggers a full model revision.

**What Would NOT Change the Rating:** One-quarter ERCOT weather headwind, CEO transition turbulence, or single delayed LOI conversion.
