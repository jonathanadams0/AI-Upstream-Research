## 2. Thesis vs. Current Valuation

**Bull case (correct, but priced):** AI creates a structural demand floor for HDDs that didn't exist before 2022. Seagate's HAMR drives (30-50TB/drive) lower the cost per TB dramatically, expanding the market. Seagate is a duopoly participant with rational pricing discipline.

**Bear case (the near-term risk):** At $927 and ~255B market cap, Seagate is priced like a hypergrowth company when the underlying HDD market grows at mid-single digits. The +500% move in 12 months price in the next several years of AI upside already.

**Our view:** Hold at current levels. The thesis is intact; the price is ahead of it. Buy below $700 where the valuation becomes more defensible on a FCF basis.
