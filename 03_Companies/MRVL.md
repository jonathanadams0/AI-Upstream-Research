# Marvell Technology (MRVL)

> Marvell designs the custom AI chips for Amazon (Trainium/Inferentia) and Microsoft (Maia), plus the PAM4 digital signal processors that enable 100G-800G optical connections inside AI data centers. The custom silicon opportunity alone — if Amazon and Microsoft execute on their AI infrastructure buildouts — represents tens of billions in revenue potential.

## 2. Thesis (3 Bullets)

1. **Amazon Trainium and Microsoft Maia are Marvell's goldmine.** AWS is investing billions to develop Trainium (AI training) and Inferentia (inference) chips to offer competitive AI cloud services without solely depending on Nvidia. Marvell co-designs these chips and manufactures the custom silicon — a long-term, high-margin partnership. Same dynamic for Microsoft's Maia chip.

2. **PAM4 DSPs for optical interconnect.** Every high-speed optical connection (100G+) inside a data center uses a PAM4 (Pulse Amplitude Modulation 4) digital signal processor to encode/decode the optical signal. Marvell is the dominant PAM4 DSP supplier, capturing revenue on every optical transceiver at AI-scale bandwidth.

3. **Data center Ethernet connectivity.** Marvell makes the PHY (physical layer) chips inside Ethernet ports across data center switches and servers. Bandwidth upgrades from 25G to 100G to 400G generate new Marvell content per port.

## 3. Investment View

Buy/$370. At $309, Marvell is at ~40x forward earnings on rapidly accelerating revenue. The custom silicon ramp is early innings — Trainium 2 and Maia are beginning their volume ramps. Each generation brings new ASP and new design cycle lock-in.
