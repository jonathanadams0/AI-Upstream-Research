## 2. Thesis (3 Bullets)

1. **AI Ethernet is winning.** Hyperscalers are standardizing on Ethernet (not InfiniBand) for their GPU clusters — the scale economics and ecosystem flexibility of Ethernet vs. Nvidia's proprietary InfiniBand are compelling. Arista is the #1 supplier of the 400G/800G switches powering these clusters.

2. **Revenue guide raised to $11.5B; stock is down.** ANET raised its annual revenue outlook to $11.5B and the stock is trading 22% below its all-time high — a disconnect that represents a buy opportunity. Analyst consensus PT is $185.72 vs. current $137.53.

3. **Recurring software revenue (EOS + CloudVision).** Arista's Extensible Operating System (EOS) and CloudVision management platform generate high-margin software subscriptions attached to each switch sold. This recurring revenue component increases earnings quality and justifies a premium multiple.

Buy/$185 (matching consensus). At $137, the pullback from $178 ATH creates a clear entry point. Q2 earnings (~July 29) are the next catalyst — watch for AI cluster customer additions and backlog update.
