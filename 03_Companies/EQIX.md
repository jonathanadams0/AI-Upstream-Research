# Equinix (EQIX)

> The global data center colocation REIT with 260+ IBX data centers in 70+ metros across 33 countries. Equinix's interconnection model — thousands of networks meeting in a single facility — is unique and creates powerful network effects that pure wholesale REITs cannot replicate. AI demand is driving new colocation bookings and retrofits of existing facilities for higher-density AI deployments.

**Sector:** [[Data-Center-Infra]] | **Rating:** Hold | **PT:** $1,150 | **Current:** ~$1,102 | **Upside:** +4%

## 2. Thesis

1. **Interconnection moat.** Equinix IBX facilities are where the internet comes together — 10,000+ networks peer at Equinix facilities. This network effect cannot be replicated by building a new data center; it requires years of attracting tenants who want to connect to each other.

2. **AI workloads need interconnection.** AI inference at the edge requires low-latency connections between hyperscaler AI compute (running the model) and enterprise data (stored at the enterprise's own network). Equinix facilities are the logical meeting point.

3. **International diversification.** Unlike DLR or IRM which are US-heavy, Equinix has a truly global footprint. AI demand is global — European, Asian, and Latin American data center demand is growing rapidly.

---

## 3. Hold Rationale

At $1,102 and ~25x EV/EBITDA, Equinix is a blue-chip REIT priced at a premium. The interconnection moat justifies it, but upside is moderate at current levels. Hold/$1,150.
