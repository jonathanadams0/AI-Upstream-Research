## 2. Thesis

1. **AI hyperscale demand is structural.** Microsoft, Google, Amazon, and Meta are all expanding data center footprints aggressively. DLR's wholesale model — building entire campuses for a single hyperscaler — is the fastest way to deploy large quantities of capacity.

2. **Pre-leased backlog.** DLR consistently pre-leases 70-80% of development pipelines before projects complete. This reduces speculative risk and provides revenue visibility.

3. **52-week high gap.** The stock at $183 vs. a 52-week high of $208 and 6-month performance of +19% represents an opportunity to buy a high-quality REIT at a 12% discount.

## 3. Bear Case

**Scenario:** New data center supply additions in key markets (Northern Virginia, Dallas, Chicago) outpace demand as hyperscalers shift to owned campuses. Interest rate increases compress REIT valuations further. Pre-leased percentage falls as smaller enterprise customers reduce footprints.

**Implied Bear Price:** $140 (~25% downside from ~$185 current)

**Change Rating to Hold/Sell If:**
1. **Pre-leased percentage on new development falls below 60% for two consecutive quarters.** Digital Realty pre-leases before building to de-risk capex. Below 60% pre-leasing signals demand softening and forces speculative construction — a significant risk change.
2. **Same-capital cash NOI growth decelerates below 3% YoY.** The REIT valuation thesis requires steady cash flow growth. Below 3% suggests the portfolio is mature and not compounding. This combined with any cap rate expansion would compress the multiple significantly.
3. **Two or more top hyperscaler customers publicly disclose a shift to owned data center campuses in DLR's core markets.** If Microsoft or Google announces a major owned campus in Northern Virginia or Dallas, it removes them from DLR's potential customer base for future leases.

**What Would NOT Change the Rating:** Single-quarter leasing delay, temporary operating expense spike, or normal interest rate volatility that doesn't reflect sustained multiple compression.
