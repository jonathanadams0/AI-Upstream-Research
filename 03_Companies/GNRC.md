# Generac Holdings (GNRC)

> Generac is the US market leader in standby and prime power generators — and data center customers now represent its fastest-growing segment. The company has a $700M data center backlog and is in "final stages of vendor approval with multiple hyperscale customers." Up 89% YTD. Buy/$320.

## 2. Thesis

1. **The grid bypass play at residential/commercial scale.** Generac's generators are smaller than CAT's (5kW-10MW range) — perfect for edge data centers, colocation facilities, and modular hyperscale deployments where 10-50 MW of backup/prime power is needed.

2. **Hyperscaler vendor approval in progress.** Generac disclosed it is in "final stages of vendor approval with multiple hyperscale customers." Once approved, contract flow can be substantial — hyperscalers standardize on approved suppliers and place repeat orders.

3. **$700M data center backlog with visibility to $1.25-1.45B EBITDA by 2028.** The financial inflection has begun. C&I segment sales up 28% YoY driven by data centers. Full-year 2026 guidance raised.

4. **Enercon acquisition enhances offering.** European grid services and power management firm — gives Generac capabilities in distributed energy resource management alongside generation.

---

## 3. Risks

1. Smaller scale than CAT — less manufacturing breadth
2. Hyperscaler vendor approval not yet complete — risk of not making the approved vendor list
3. Residential generator business (Generac's legacy) faces competition from Tesla Powerwall, Sunrun — but this is separate from the C&I/data center thesis

## 4. Bear Case

**Scenario:** Hyperscaler generator approvals stall in regulatory/permitting process. Generac's AI data center opportunity fails to materialize at the scale management guided. Residential standby market remains soft due to high interest rates suppressing home improvement spending.

**Implied Bear Price:** $175 (~37% downside from ~$278 current)

**Change Rating to Hold/Sell If:**
1. **No hyperscaler generator approval converts to purchase order within 9 months.** Management has disclosed ongoing discussions for large-scale AI data center generator deployments. If by Q1 2027 no named hyperscaler order is announced, the data center thesis has not converted.
2. **Residential product revenues decline more than 20% YoY for two consecutive quarters.** The residential standby business is Generac's margin anchor. Sustained weakness signals the AI tailwind isn't offsetting core market headwinds.
3. **Gross margin sustained below 35%.** Current margins have been suppressed by supply chain normalization. If they don't recover to historical 38-40% range by Q2 2027, pricing power is structurally impaired.

**What Would NOT Change the Rating:** Single-quarter residential weather miss, temporary supply chain delay, or one delayed data center approval.
