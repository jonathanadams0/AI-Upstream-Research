## 2. Context

AECOM is one of the world's largest engineering and construction services firms. It designs, builds, and manages complex infrastructure: roads, bridges, water systems, airports, military bases, and data centers.

**The AI data center connection:** Every large AI campus requires engineering design, permitting support, civil construction, and MEP (mechanical, electrical, plumbing) engineering. AECOM competes for these contracts alongside Fluor, Jacobs, and Turner/Mortenson (private).

**Why the stock is down while the thesis should be up:** AECOM's government/public sector exposure (roads, military, municipal water) may be weighing on sentiment amid budget uncertainty. Data center engineering is growing rapidly within AECOM but hasn't yet been broken out as a segment.
