## 2. Thesis

1. **AI servers are inside every major AI server.** MPWR power ICs are a bill-of-materials staple in Dell, HPE, and Supermicro AI servers. Every GPU sold eventually needs MPWR's VRMs.

2. **48V migration is a catalyst.** As data centers migrate from 12V to 48V bus architecture, new power conversion stages are needed — and MPWR's portfolio is positioned for this transition.

3. **Margin excellence.** MPWR consistently earns 55%+ gross margins — exceptional for analog semiconductors. The proprietary power IC design process creates sticky customer relationships.
