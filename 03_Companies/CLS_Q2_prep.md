**Q1 — Customer concentration (primary invalidation gate)**

If management provides customer concentration data or 10-Q Note disclosures reveal a ≥40% concentration, evaluate immediately against the invalidation trigger. No data = no trigger, but flag for 10-Q filing review.

**Q2 — CCS Enterprise revenue execution vs. +130% guide**
Q2 was explicitly guided to +130% YoY growth in CCS enterprise. This is the AI server manufacturing thesis in numeric form. A miss here (say, +80% or below) would signal either timing delays or hyperscaler order deferral and would compress both the revenue multiple and conviction. Beat or in-line is thesis confirmation.

**Q3 — Gross margin trajectory (bear trigger #2)**
Bear case trigger: gross margin below 7% for two consecutive quarters. CLS runs thin margins (~10-11%). Watch for any pricing concession commentary or volume deleverage. If Q2 gross margin compresses toward 7-8%, flag — the bear case activates with a second sequential quarter at that level.
