## Thesis Recap

Cameco is the largest Western uranium miner, with world-class Cigar Lake and McArthur River assets in Saskatchewan, Canada. The nuclear renaissance thesis has three legs:

1. **Uranium demand step-up from nuclear restarts + SMRs.** TMI restart (CEG/Microsoft), Palisades restart (Michigan), Comanche Peak uprates (VST/AWS), SMR pipeline (GEV BWRX-300). Each reactor restart or new build increases long-term uranium enrichment demand.

2. **Westinghouse JV creates full fuel cycle exposure.** Cameco co-owns Westinghouse (with Brookfield Asset Management) — the world's leading nuclear technology company. Westinghouse provides fuel fabrication, engineering services, AP1000 reactor technology, and services to ~50% of global nuclear capacity. This transforms CCJ from a pure mining play to an integrated nuclear fuel cycle company.

3. **Long-term contracting at above-spot prices.** Cameco is sold out through 2028+ on long-term contracts signed at prices well above the historical average. The realized price of $66.21/lb in Q1'26 vs. a spot price that has been volatile reflects the value of Cameco's contracted book.
