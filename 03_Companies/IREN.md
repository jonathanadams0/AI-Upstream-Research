# IREN Limited (IREN)

> IREN (formerly Iris Energy) is an Australian-founded, Nasdaq-listed operator of Bitcoin mining and AI cloud data centers. Pivoting from pure mining to GPU cloud hosting, IREN is one of the Aschenbrenner "miners to AI hosts" basket names — power infrastructure repurposed for AI compute.

**Sector:** [[Data-Center-Infra]] | **Rating:** Hold | **PT:** $72 | **Current:** ~$67 | **Upside:** +8%

## 2. Thesis

IREN's existing Bitcoin mining infrastructure includes significant power contracts and owned/leased real estate in low-cost power markets (Canada, Texas). The pivot thesis is that GPU cloud revenues (renting H100/H200 time to AI companies) carry better economics and more predictable revenue than Bitcoin mining. Aschenbrenner held IREN as part of the AI hosting basket.

**Hold rationale:** At $67 with 8% upside to PT, risk/reward is balanced. The company is mid-pivot — GPU revenues are growing but mining remains a significant share. Would become Buy if a major AI cloud contract (like CoreWeave has with CORZ) is announced.
