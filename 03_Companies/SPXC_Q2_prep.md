## Invalidation Trigger

**Thesis Invalidation Tracker row (verbatim):** "Sell Trigger: HVAC segment bookings decline >20% for 2 quarters | Monitor Via: Quarterly bookings."

**Would this be the 1st or 2nd confirming quarter?** The 1st. SPXC.md does not disclose an actual HVAC bookings figure or trend anywhere in the note (only revenue, EBITDA, CapEx, and debt series are given) — there is no documented prior quarter already showing a qualifying >20% bookings decline. If Q2 bookings print a decline exceeding 20% YoY, this would be the **first** confirming quarter, starting the two-quarter clock toward the sell trigger, not completing it.

**What in this print would fire it:** A disclosed HVAC segment bookings decline greater than 20% YoY. Given no baseline bookings trend is in the note, any bookings disclosure this quarter is itself new information — watch specifically for whether management frames bookings growth as decelerating-but-positive (consistent with thesis) vs. outright negative at scale (early trigger signal).
